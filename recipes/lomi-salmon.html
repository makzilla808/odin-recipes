Lomi Salmon

While not exactly super tradish this Hawaiian dish is still a staple. A concoction created by Hawaiian sailors is a simple enough dish made of salted salmon, tomatoes and onions. An iconic side dish.

Ingredients

The amount of each of the ingredients depends on preference as well.

  • 1 pound salted salmon
  • 4 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small sweet onion, diced
  • 3-4 green onions, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Prep
    • Place the salted salmon in cold water.
    • Let it soak for 2 hours to reduce the saltiness.
    • Taste a small piece to check the salt. If it is still to salty, soak it for an additional hour.
    • Dice and chop all your ingredients

  2. Assembly
    • In a large bowl, combine the diced salmon, tomatoes, sweet onions and green onions.
    • Mix together all the ingredients. Be careful not to mash up the tomatoes.

  3. Serving
    • Let it sit for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
    • Optional: adding some ice to the bowl to keep it cold and refreshing.
    • Serve as a side with poi, laulau and kalua pig. Or just mix it in with some poi. So good!
